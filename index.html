The four-day rabbit hole

iOS Safari染色 chrome with whatever sits at the viewport edge — but the rules are quirky, undocumented, and shift between versions:

  • Viewport bottom染色 mint while your belt section is dark teal — visible seam.
  • Compact tab bar appears; chrome染色 "shifts" 30px because 100lvh − 100svh ≠ current chrome height.
  • User pulls past footer — rubber-band overscroll exposes html bg, the wrong color.
  • theme-color meta is ignored on iOS 26.
  • body::before with fixed gradient stretches into the overscroll exposed area.

bleed walks the rabbit hole for you.
